## Key Concepts

### Chapter 1: Capillarity Fundamentals

#### Surface Tension
$$\gamma = \frac{dF}{dA}$$
Force per unit length (N/m) or energy per unit area (J/m²)

#### Young-Laplace Equation
$$\Delta P = \gamma \left(\frac{1}{R_1} + \frac{1}{R_2}\right)$$

#### Young's Equation (Contact Angle)
$$\gamma_{SV} = \gamma_{SL} + \gamma_{LV}\cos\theta$$

### Chapter 2: Capillarity and Gravity

#### Capillary Length
$$\kappa^{-1} = \sqrt{\frac{\gamma}{\rho g}}$$

For water: $\kappa^{-1} \approx 2.7$ mm

#### Bond Number
$$Bo = \frac{\rho g L^2}{\gamma}$$

### Chapter 5: Thin Films

#### Film on Solid Substrate
- Lubrication approximation
- Viscous flow in thin layer
- Pressure-driven drainage

#### Film on Liquid Substrate
- Floating films
- Two free surfaces

### Chapter 7: Dewetting

#### Viscous Dewetting Velocity
$$V \sim \frac{\gamma \theta^3}{\eta}$$

#### Spinodal Dewetting
Unstable films spontaneously rupture when:
$$h < h_c \sim \left(\frac{A}{\gamma}\right)^{1/2}$$
where $A$ is Hamaker constant.

### Chapter 8: Surfactants & Soap Films

#### Surfactant Structure
- Hydrophilic head + Hydrophobic tail
- Adsorb at interfaces
- Reduce surface tension

#### Marangoni Effect
Surface tension gradients drive flow:
$$\tau = \frac{\partial\gamma}{\partial x}$$

#### Soap Film Drainage
- Gravitational drainage
- Capillary suction at Plateau borders
- Marginal regeneration

## Key Equations for Soap Bubbles

### Pressure Inside Bubble
$$P_{inside} - P_{outside} = \frac{4\gamma}{R}$$
(Factor of 4 because two surfaces)

### Plateau Border Suction
$$\Delta P_{border} \sim \frac{\gamma}{r}$$
where $r$ is border radius of curvature.

### Drainage Rate (simplified)
$$\frac{dh}{dt} \propto -\frac{\rho g h^2}{\eta}$$

### Film Lifetime
$$\tau \sim \frac{\eta}{\rho g h_0}$$
