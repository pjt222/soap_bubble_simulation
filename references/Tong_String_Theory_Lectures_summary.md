## Key Concepts Relevant to Soap Bubbles

### The Classical String (Chapter 1)

#### Nambu-Goto Action
String worldsheet area minimization:
$$S_{NG} = -T \int d^2\sigma \sqrt{-\det(\partial_\alpha X^\mu \partial_\beta X_\mu)}$$
where $T$ is string tension, $\sigma$ are worldsheet coordinates, $X^\mu$ are spacetime coordinates.

- **Analogy**: String minimizes worldsheet area ↔ Soap film minimizes surface area
- Both governed by tension × area energy functional

#### Polyakov Action
Equivalent formulation with auxiliary worldsheet metric:
$$S_P = -\frac{T}{2} \int d^2\sigma \sqrt{-h} h^{\alpha\beta} \partial_\alpha X^\mu \partial_\beta X_\mu$$

### Worldsheet as Minimal Surface
- String sweeps out 2D surface (worldsheet) in spacetime
- Classical solutions minimize worldsheet area
- Equations of motion: wave equation on worldsheet
- Boundary conditions determine open vs closed strings

### Conformal Field Theory (Chapter 4)
- Worldsheet physics described by 2D CFT
- Scale invariance and conformal symmetry
- Relevant for understanding fluctuations on surfaces
